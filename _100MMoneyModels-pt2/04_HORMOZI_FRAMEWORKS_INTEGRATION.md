## 1. FAST CASH FRAMEWORK
### *"A quick nickel beats a slow dime when you know how to use it"*

**Core Concept**: Limited-time offers to warm audiences (current/previous customers, engaged leads)

**Velocity Audit Application**:
- Use as **Phase 0** before full system build
- Extract 30-day cash to fund Momentum Stack implementation

**Fast Cash Sequence**:
1. Announcement email to existing customer base
2. Recap of offer value
3. Two days out reminder
4. 24 hours out final call
5. Cart close

**Velocity Audit Application**:
